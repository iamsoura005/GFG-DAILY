A thief can carry at most </span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: bold; font-variant: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">t </span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-variant: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">plates in total. He may take any number of plates from a box, but cannot take more plates than are available in that box. </span></span></p>
<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-11965e31-7fff-59d3-e9e7-00a868454c20" style="font-size: 14pt;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-variant: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Return the <strong>maximum</strong> number of gold coins the thief can steal.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="font-size: 18px;">Input:</strong> </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">t =<strong> </strong>3,</span> <span style="font-size: 18px;">a[] = [1, 2, 3], b[] = [3, 2, 1]</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Output:</span> </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">7</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The thief takes 1 plate from the first box and 2 plates from the second box.<br>Total gold coins stolen = (1 × 3) + (2 × 2) = 7.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Input:</span> </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">t = 0, </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">a[] = [1, 3, 2]</span><span style="font-size: 18px;">, </span><span style="font-size: 18px;">b[] = [2, 3, 1]</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Output:</span> </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">0</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The thief cannot carry any plates, so he steals 0 gold coins.</span></pre>
